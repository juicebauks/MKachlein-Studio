Flag Series

A reflection on the evolving symbolism of the American flag, this series explores its power to unite and divide in a rapidly shifting national landscape.

Floral Shield Series

Transforming tropical leaves into striking metal shields, this series highlights the intricate patterns and bold forms of nature’s most vibrant creations.

Mask Series

Whimsical yet profound, these characters examine the fluid nature of identity, revealing the hidden stories we wear throughout our lives.

Rock Stack Series

A tribute to cairns and the instinct to leave a mark, these balanced sculptures celebrate guidance, play, and the art of finding the way.

Skull Series

Inspired by sun-bleached animal skulls, this series captures the raw beauty, resilience, and fragility of life in unforgiving landscapes.

Something to Say Series

This series transforms children's drawings and messages into lasting steel sculptures, preserving the honesty, humor, and creativity of their original artwork.
