Mask Series

2016 - 2018

The concept of wearing different masks throughout our lives, depending on our roles at the time, has always intrigued me. While masks often symbolize adornment or theatrical disguise, I approached this series with a deeper exploration of identity and expression. Though the masks may appear whimsical, their design and movement reflect a more complex dialogue that emerged during their creation. Each character, with its own voice, had a story to tell as it took shape.
